About Us

A virtual private networking software in the works.

Our Mission
Change the game through purpose.

Exordium is an upcoming virtual private networking software, whose main focus is around providing our customers with privacy and security while browsing the internet, and doing what we can to assist with environmental protection, and the fight against the climate crisis.

How We Started

In December 2018, "Gannicus" the founder of the Exordium Project, wanted to combine his technological knowledge with his love for the natural environment.

While the virtual private network is a form of protection for people all around the world who are experiencing censorship, content control, intrusive government surveillance, and hackers, it is also a form of making some good through technology. Exordium's mission is to provide an awesome service for its customers, and do what it can to give profits made towards environmental protections and the fight against climate change.
